FOODS

Guwahati is where much of Assam's city glitter resides. So, while most of the other towns in the state will offer you local preparations in local food joints and a few restaurants, Guwahati sparkles with choices only a commercially developed city can offer. Guwahati sustains the local Assamese foods in its menu such as Nga-Thongba which is a fish and ginger curry, Erongba, a jackfruit-kernel chutney, Laksa and a lot more, and at the same time offers everything from Indian, Mughlai, Chinese to Continental cuisines and a few more. Indulge yourself in the pastas, pizzas, cakes and pasteries widely available in the area. You can also relax and enjoy in a number of bars and pubs that the city has to offer.

Restaurants in Guwahati

  1. Khorikaa


    Assamese
    10:30 AM to 4 PM, 7 PM to 10:30 PM
    INR 500 for two
    This place serves some finely prepared Assamese dishes. A must place visit to enjoy the local cuisine.
    1st Floor, Kamal C Plaza, Bora Service, G S Road, Ulubari, Guwahati, India

  2. Paradise


    Assamese
    10:30 AM to 10:30 PM
    INR 550 for two
    Paradise is one of the oldest restaurants in Guwahati and is known for it's amazing Assamese cuisine. The quality of food has been maintained for years. If you are a non-vegetarian, do not forget to try the non-veg thali. You will love it.
    Paradise Hotel, Near Goswami Service, MRD Road, Silpukhuri, Guwahati, India

  3. Tandoor


    Cafe
    12:30PM-3PM & 7PM-11:30PM
    INR 500 for two
    Tandoor is situated in Dynasty Hotel on Sir Shahdullah (SS) road. With a differently cultured ambiance, Tandoori provides sheer pleasure to the visitors. To enjoy the luxurious experience with some soft tanpura music, you must visit this place. Do not forget to try various types of Kebabs they offer. They're truly one of a kind,
    Dynasty Hotel,SS Road,Lakhtokia,Guwahati, Assam 781001

  4. Beatrix


    Chinese, North-Indian
